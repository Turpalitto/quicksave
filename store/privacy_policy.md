# QuickSave — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 21, 2026

QuickSave ("we", "the app") helps you download **public** Instagram media you explicitly choose to save.

## What we collect

- **URLs you share or paste** — sent to the resolver to obtain public media links.
- **App settings and download history** — stored locally on your device.
- **We do not** require Instagram login or collect passwords.

## QuickSave Cloud (hosted resolver)

When using the default cloud resolver, your Instagram URL is transmitted to our server only to resolve public media. We do not store your downloaded files on our servers. Resolver requests may be logged for reliability and abuse prevention (IP, URL hash, timestamp).

## Self-hosted mode

In self-hosted mode, URLs are sent only to **your** backend server.

## Permissions

- **Internet** — resolve and download media
- **Notifications** — download completion (optional)
- **Storage / Media** — save files to Gallery when enabled

## Children's privacy

QuickSave is not directed at children under 13.
